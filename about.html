About AI Income Lab

AI Income Lab · Established 2025

AI Income Lab is a research and publishing project focused on one question: how do you actually build sustainable income using AI tools?

There's a lot of noise online about making money with AI. Most of it is hype — "10x your income overnight!" type content that doesn't hold up to scrutiny. This site aims to be different: practical, honest, and grounded in what actually works.

What We Cover

  • Affiliate marketing with AI — which programs are worth joining, how to write reviews that convert, building long-term passive income
  • AI tool reviews — honest comparisons of writing, image, and automation tools with real-world testing
  • Freelancing with AI — practical guides to offering services on Fiverr and Upwork using AI assistance
  • SEO and content strategy — how to get organic traffic without a massive budget
  • Passive income streams — digital products, niche sites, automated systems that generate income while you sleep

Our Approach

Content on this site is researched and drafted with AI assistance, then reviewed and edited by a human before publication. We don't publish raw AI output — every article goes through an editorial process to ensure accuracy, original insight, and genuine usefulness.

We follow Google's guidelines on helpful content: every article should answer a real question better than the alternatives. If an article doesn't meet that bar, it doesn't get published.
